What is expected of a cleaning lady?

### **1\. Common Duties of a Cleaning Lady**

A cleaning lady is typically responsible for a wide range of tasks depending on your home’s needs:

-   **Dusting and wiping surfaces:** Furniture, shelves, countertops, and décor
    
-   **Vacuuming and mopping floors:** Carpets, rugs, and hard floors
    
-   **Bathroom cleaning:** Toilets, sinks, showers, mirrors, and floors
    
-   **Kitchen cleaning:** Countertops, sinks, stovetops, and appliance exteriors
    
-   **Trash removal:** Emptying bins and recycling as needed
    
-   **Tidying and organizing:** Straightening up rooms, making beds, and arranging items
    

Some cleaning ladies may also offer **additional services** such as laundry, dishwashing, or organizing closets, depending on your agreement.

### **2\. Professionalism and Reliability**

A professional cleaning lady is expected to be:

-   **Punctual:** Arriving on time for scheduled cleaning sessions
    
-   **Trustworthy:** Handling keys, access, and personal spaces with care
    
-   **Efficient:** Completing tasks thoroughly within the allotted time
    
-   **Detail-oriented:** Paying attention to small areas that are often overlooked
    
-   **Flexible:** Adjusting to your home’s specific needs and preferences
    

Professional **house cleaning services near me** follow these standards consistently to provide dependable and high-quality service.

### **3\. Communication and Expectations**

Clear communication ensures a smooth cleaning experience:

-   Discuss which areas require focus or special attention
    
-   Specify cleaning products if you have preferences
    
-   Share scheduling needs and any restrictions
    
-   Establish payment terms and frequency of service

-   **Maintains cleanliness:** Regular cleaning prevents dirt, dust, and bacteria buildup
    
-   **Reduces stress:** A clean home contributes to better mental health and comfort
    
-   **Ensures thorough cleaning:** Professionals know the best techniques and tools
