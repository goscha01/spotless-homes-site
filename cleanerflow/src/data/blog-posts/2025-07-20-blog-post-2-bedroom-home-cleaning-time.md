### 🕒 Average Cleaning Time of a 2-Bedroom Home

Typically, a standard cleaning for a 2-bedroom, 1–2 bathroom home takes around **2 to 3.5 hours** with one professional cleaner. This can vary depending on the level of cleanliness, the presence of pets, square footage, and additional services requested.

**Factors that may increase cleaning time:**

-   Extra bathrooms
    
-   Kitchen appliances needing deep cleaning
    
-   Heavy buildup (first-time or move-in/out)
    
-   Cluttered or heavily furnished rooms

### 🧼 Deep Cleaning vs. Standard Cleaning

**Standard Cleaning:**Great for regular upkeep. Includes dusting, vacuuming, wiping surfaces, and basic bathroom and kitchen cleaning.

**Deep Cleaning:**Takes more time—usually **3.5 to 5 hours**—and covers inside appliances, baseboards, high-touch areas, and more.

💡 First-time clients usually need a deep cleaning before switching to regular service.
