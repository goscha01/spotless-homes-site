# How Much Do Most Cleaning Services Charge?

When you are hiring a cleaning service, one of the first things that cross your mind is How Much Do Most Cleaning Services Charge?. The prices for cleaning services can be extremely varied based on many variables, including the type of service you require, the size of your property, how much cleaning is required, and where you are located. Let's break down the average prices so you'll have an idea of what to expect.

![How Much Do Most Cleaning Services Charge?](/assets/blog/how-much-do-most-cleaning-services-charge-01.png)

How Much Do Most Cleaning Services Charge?

1\. Types of Cleaning Services and Prices

Basic Cleaning: This is typically dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general tidying up. Prices ranging from $100 to $150 for a 2-bedroom, 1-bathroom home, and hourly rates of around $30 to $50.

Deep Cleaning: Deep cleaning goes beyond the regular cleaning and touches areas that are often neglected. Some of the areas involve scrubbing the bathrooms, dusting furniture legs, and degrading encrustation. The charges can range from $150 to $300, depending on the size of the property and the amount of cleaning required.

Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning: This service is more comprehensive, and there is deep cleaning of all rooms, appliances, baseboards, and closets. Prices will usually be between $200 and can go as high as $400 or more.

Recurring Cleaning: Most cleaning services are willing to offer discounts for regular cleanings. Bi-weekly or weekly cleaning will cost anywhere from $80 to $120 per session, depending on property size and condition.

2\. Factors that Affect Cleaning Service Prices

Property Size and Condition: Larger houses and properties in a bad condition will take longer and more effort and therefore cost more.

Pets: Pet houses may be more expensive to remove pet hair and odors.

Special Requests: Extra services like window cleaning, laundry, and cleaning may cost $20 to $50 per service.

Location: The pricing may be differential according to living expenses in the region and area competition.

3\. Maximizing the Value

Get an Estimate: Get a full estimate before hiring a cleaning service. Make sure it includes all the possible fees.

Choose Regular Services: Many companies will offer a discount for frequent customers.

Group Services: Grouping services like deep cleaning and window cleaning can sometimes lower the overall cost.

Understand the cost structure so you can make an educated decision and receive the most quality cleaning service suitable for your budget and requirements.
